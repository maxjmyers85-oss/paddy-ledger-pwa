======================================
  PADDY LEDGER — PWA Setup Guide
======================================

WHAT THIS IS
------------
A web version of Paddy Ledger that anyone can use on their phone or
computer by visiting a link — no install required.

STEP 1 — Create a free GitHub account
--------------------------------------
Go to https://github.com and sign up (free).

STEP 2 — Upload these files to GitHub
--------------------------------------
1. On GitHub, click the "+" icon → "New repository"
2. Name it "paddy-ledger-pwa", set to Public, click "Create repository"
3. Click "uploading an existing file"
4. Drag and drop ALL files from this folder into the browser
   IMPORTANT: Also drag the "src" and "public" subfolders
5. Click "Commit changes"

STEP 3 — Deploy with Vercel (free)
------------------------------------
1. Go to https://vercel.com and sign up with your GitHub account
2. Click "Add New Project"
3. Find "paddy-ledger-pwa" and click Import
4. Leave all settings as default, click Deploy
5. Wait ~1 minute — Vercel gives you a live URL like:
   https://paddy-ledger-pwa.vercel.app

STEP 4 — Share with your friend
---------------------------------
Text him the Vercel URL. On his iPhone:
1. Open the link in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"
It now sits on his home screen like a real app, works offline,
and his data saves automatically to his phone.

NOTES
------
- Each person's data is stored separately on their own device
- Works on iPhone, Android, and any computer browser
- Free to host, no monthly fees
- To update the app: replace files on GitHub, Vercel auto-deploys
